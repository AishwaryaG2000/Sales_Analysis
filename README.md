# Sales_Analysis
This project involves **Sales Analysis** to design an effective Marketing Campaign, and analyze the Diwali sales data to identify customer purchasing behavior across demographics (age, gender, marital status, occupation, and region) and product categories. The goal is to discover key
patterns that can help optimize marketing strategies, improve customer targeting, and maximize
sales revenue during festive seasons.

- **Gender** wise contribution in sales
- Most profitable **States** and **Zones** in India
- **Age group** contributing in the sales
- The most sold **Product Categories**
- **Occupation** of the most contributing customers

## 🎯 Project Objective
The primary objective of this project is to:

1. Identify which age groups and genders contribute most to sales.
2. Determine the most profitable states/zones for Diwali sales.
3. Find top-performing occupations and their buying capacity.
4. Highlight best-selling product categories.
5. Provide insights for targeted marketing campaigns.

## 🧠 Key Business Insights
- **Women** are your core decision-makers + buyers, especially for lifestyle and daily-use categories
- The primary buyers are working professionals with disposable income, not seniors or teens
- **White-collar, salaried** customers have higher AOV (average order value)
- Customers prefer essentials, lifestyle products, and impulse buys
- Not all regions are equally cost-efficient, **Central and Southern Zones** showed the highest sales

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Bundle & cross-sell for higher cart value
- Salary Week Offers
- Workweek Essentials Sale
- Corporate Employee Discounts
